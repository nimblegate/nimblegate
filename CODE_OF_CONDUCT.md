# Code of Conduct

nimblegate's project spaces - Issues, Discussions, pull requests, and any other
project channel - exist to help people use and improve the tool. This document
sets the minimum expectations so those spaces stay useful, and gives maintainers
a clear basis to act when they are not met.

## Expected

- Be respectful and assume good faith. Disagree about ideas, not people.
- Stay on topic and keep posts relevant to the project.
- Search before asking, and help others where you reasonably can.

## Not acceptable

- Spam, advertising, link-dropping, or off-topic flooding.
- Harassment, personal attacks, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Posting other people's private information.
- Deliberately derailing threads, or repeated low-effort or automated noise.
- Posting security vulnerabilities publicly - report those privately instead
  (see [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md)).

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove or edit content, lock or delete threads, and warn, block,
or ban accounts that violate this document - at their discretion, with or without
prior notice. The aim is to keep the project's spaces useful, not to punish.

## Reporting

See a problem? Email `conduct@nimblegate.com`. Reports are handled privately.
This document applies to everyone in the project's spaces, maintainers included;
a report about a maintainer goes to the same address.
